So you think you can design?

The court of popular opinion has it that for one to enter into the world of tech, the easiest way is through design. The court may be right, but the truth is not everyone can design.

Strong communication abilities, the capacity for teamwork, and an eye for detail are requirements for the job. Designers can also benefit from hard skills like software design proficiency, and a thorough understanding of color. In my expeience, I believe every aspirin designer should have a good experiencein the following skills: Layout and navigation, Color and typography, Design principles, Mobile-first design, Design software and HTML, CSS, or JQuery.

Why are wireframes and sketchs so important in digital design?

I’ve been a graphics deigner for close to 15 years and even with my level of expertise, I still don’t like sketching my designs. Yes I’m bad at drawing, and sometimes I feel its a complete waste of time. But without these few sribbles with pencil, paper and sometimes digital tools, a true designer would be lost, and sometimes deliver short of the expected outcome.

By focusing on usability rather than aesthetics, wireframes assist you. Wireframes are a useful tool for displaying screen layouts and their functional components. You may evaluate a product's usability, layouts, and functionality objectively by using wireframes during the early stages of development. Ever come up with a concept for a website or application? It's simple to have an idea, but comprehending how it will appear in a user interface is challenging. Sketching is helpful in this situation. Many creative and construction-related vocations include sketching.
